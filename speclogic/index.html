Capabilities

SpecLogic Modules

RFQ Intelligence

When a customer requests a quote, SpecLogic pulls from your historical pricing, material costs, and production capacity to generate accurate quotes fast — cutting your quote-to-order cycle and helping you win more business.

Supplier Scoring

Builds a living scorecard for every supplier based on pricing accuracy, delivery performance, quality history, and responsiveness. Institutional knowledge that stays when people leave.

Spec Compliance

Automatically validates supplier quotes against your material specifications, tolerance requirements, and certification standards. Catches substitutions and non-conformances before they reach the floor.

Cost Intelligence

Tracks material pricing trends across suppliers and commodity indices. Flags anomalies, identifies negotiation opportunities, and benchmarks every quote against your historical data.

Sales-to-Delivery Tracking

Connects the customer quote through supplier sourcing to production delivery in real-time. Track margin drift, lead-time slippage, and fulfillment risk before they become problems.
