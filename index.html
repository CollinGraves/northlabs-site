Our Thesis

Industrial companies should own their intelligence

"The current model is broken. Your operational data, supplier performance, production history—it all flows into software that gives you dashboards but keeps the intelligence."

Your data is your most valuable asset

Every job you run, every supplier you evaluate, every outcome you track generates operational intelligence. But today's tools capture that value for themselves—your insights train their models, improve their products, and get sold back to your competitors.

AI should be infrastructure, not subscription

You own your equipment. You own your trucks. You should own your AI. We build intelligence systems that become assets on your balance sheet—compounding value over time, not monthly fees that disappear when you stop paying.

Embedded beats bolted-on

Dashboards don't change behavior. Tools people have to remember to check don't get checked. Real intelligence shows up where decisions happen—in your email, your meetings, your existing workflows.
