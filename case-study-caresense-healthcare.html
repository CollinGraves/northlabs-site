Healthcare

30% Faster Clinical Reconciliation with CareSense

How North Labs deployed CareSense to transform healthcare operations—delivering clinical intelligence to the right person, at the right time, enabling the right decisions for both patient care and business performance.

The Challenge

A regional healthcare provider was grappling with the triple threat facing mid-market health systems: staffing turnover draining institutional knowledge, siloed data across clinical and revenue cycle departments, and manual reconciliation processes consuming hundreds of staff hours each month.

With administrative turnover reaching nearly 40% annually, critical operational knowledge was walking out the door. Revenue cycle teams relied on tribal knowledge and fragmented spreadsheets, making clinical reconciliation slow, error-prone, and impossible to scale.

The institution needed a system of intelligence—not just reporting—that could capture institutional knowledge, automate reconciliation workflows, and deliver actionable insights regardless of staff turnover.

Business Impact

30% Faster Reconciliation

Clinical reconciliation cycle times reduced by 30% through automated workflows and intelligent data matching across systems.

Turnover-Resilient Operations

Institutional knowledge captured in the intelligence layer, ensuring operational continuity regardless of staff changes.

Intelligence at the Point of Decision

Right information delivered to the right person at the right time—transforming reactive reporting into proactive decision-making.

The Solution

North Labs deployed CareSense as a comprehensive system of intelligence for the healthcare provider. Rather than layering another reporting tool on top of existing silos, CareSense was built to serve as the operational backbone—ingesting data from clinical, financial, and administrative systems into a unified intelligence layer.

The engagement followed a fractionalized Chief Data Officer (CDO) approach: North Labs handled the heavy lifting of data transformation, business logic mapping, and stakeholder alignment, while ensuring the institution maintained full ownership of their data and intellectual property through tenant-isolated, HIPAA-compliant deployment.

Automated reconciliation workflows replaced manual cross-referencing, while intelligent alerting ensured that exceptions and anomalies surfaced to the right team members in real time.

"Intelligence delivered to the right person at the right time—that's what CareSense means for our organization. We finally have operational resilience."

— VP of Operations, Regional Healthcare Provider

The Outcome

The healthcare provider now operates with a clinical intelligence platform that has reduced reconciliation time by 30%, captured institutional knowledge in a durable system, and empowered staff to focus on high-value patient care rather than manual data wrangling.

CareSense continues to expand across the organization, with new use cases in denials management, staffing optimization, and predictive analytics for patient volume forecasting—each module building on the foundational intelligence layer.
