Construction & Heavy Civil

74.5% Faster Bid Turnaround with TradeSmith

How North Labs helped a leading construction company transform bid analysis from a days-long manual process into an intelligence-driven workflow—cutting turnaround time by 74.5%.

"Reduced our bid turnaround from days to hours. The speed and accuracy have fundamentally changed how we compete."

— VP of Estimating, Construction & Heavy Civil Company

The Challenge

A mid-market construction and heavy civil firm was losing competitive bids due to slow turnaround times. Their estimating team relied on manual spreadsheet processes, tribal knowledge, and disconnected data sources to analyze incoming bid opportunities. Each bid required cross-referencing historical project data, material pricing, labor rates, and subcontractor availability—a process that took days.

Leadership recognized that the speed of their bid response was directly tied to win rate and revenue growth, but lacked the data infrastructure to accelerate the process without sacrificing accuracy.

The core problem: a highly manual, spreadsheet-driven bid analysis process that was too slow to compete in an increasingly fast-moving market.

Business Impact

74.5% Reduction in Bid Analysis Time

What previously took days was reduced to hours through automated data aggregation and intelligent analysis workflows.

Increased Win Rate

Faster, more accurate bids enabled the company to respond to more opportunities and improve their competitive positioning.

Production-Ready in Under 6 Months

From discovery through reiteration, the solution reached production with validated ROI within two quarters of engagement.

The Solution

North Labs conducted deep discovery through stakeholder interviews across the estimating, operations, and executive teams. Bid analysis emerged as the highest-ROI opportunity—a process ripe for automation and intelligence.

A lightweight application was rolled out to two test users within 30 days of gaining access. Prioritizing throughput and continual idea refinement, the team reached a 4th version with early test users within the first quarter. By the end of the second quarter, the solution was in production with validated ROI.

The platform, now branded as TradeSmith, integrates historical bid data, material costs, labor models, and market signals into a unified intelligence layer—purpose-built for trade-driven industries.

The Outcome

The construction company now operates with a data-driven bid analysis workflow that has cut turnaround time by 74.5%. The estimating team can respond to more bids with greater accuracy, directly improving win rates and revenue.

Following the success of bid analysis, the engagement has expanded into estimation, WIP visibility, and sales operations—a continued investment in the TradeSmith platform that reflects the depth of trust built through measurable results.
