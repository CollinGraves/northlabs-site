Culmination Bio harnesses data to influence precision medicine

How North Labs helped a biotech company unlock 25 years of patient data to accelerate progress in precision medicine research and outcomes.

"North Labs has been a reliable and competent partner for using AWS QuickSight."

— SVP of Data, Culmination Bio

The Challenge

Culmination Bio, a prominent biotech company, strategically harnesses longitudinal comprehensive datasets across the patient journey. Their purpose is to pioneer the creation of groundbreaking diagnostic tests, facilitate the implementation of cutting-edge therapeutic solutions, and deliver care process models. Furthermore, they seek to provide clinical insights, pinpoint therapeutic targets, and formulate meticulous care process models.

Over the course of its operation, the organization had refined an extensive repository of data, totaling hundreds of gigabytes. This remarkable collection spanned a quarter-century of patient history — covering geographic distribution, diagnoses, prescribed medications, procedures undergone, laboratory results, allergies, and even genomic information.

The problem? The customer had all of this data but no way to display it — no way to demonstrate the depth of their datasets or the insights they could unlock for pharmaceutical firms and researchers.

Business Impact

25 Years of Patient Data Unlocked

Hundreds of gigabytes of longitudinal patient data made accessible and queryable for the first time through interactive dashboards.

Precision Medicine Accelerated

Enabled the biotech enterprise to demonstrate both the depth of their data and the insights that piqued the interest of pharmaceutical firms and researchers.

Opportunities Identified

The client became adept at pinpointing opportunities for advancing healthcare and medicine, solidifying their pivotal role in the industry's progress.

The Solution

North Labs was brought in to help accelerate the capabilities of the customer's large, complex volume of data. Amazon QuickSight was selected for a multitude of reasons. The customer had their data in Amazon S3 and Athena, which would allow for ease of ingestion. Amazon QuickSight's in-memory engine, SPICE, responds with remarkable speed and offers interactive dashboards, embedded analytics, and natural language queries.

As the project evolved, North Labs adapted to expanding requirements — ultimately helping the biotech team demonstrate both the depth of their data and the kinds of insights that attracted interest from pharmaceutical firms and researchers.

The Outcome

Through this collaboration, Culmination Bio gained the ability to identify opportunities for advancing healthcare and medicine — strengthening their position in the industry.

With better visibility and access to their data, Culmination Bio accelerated their understanding of diagnostics and therapeutics performance, bringing the promise of precision medicine closer to reality.
