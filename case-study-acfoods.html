Food Manufacturing

Executive Insights Across Product Lines for AC Foods

How North Labs built AC Foods a unified executive intelligence platform with drill-down capability across separate product lines, natural language querying, and real-time performance visibility for leadership.

"Drill-down capability and natural language querying changed how we operate. We went from monthly report reviews to real-time decision-making across every product line."

— CEO, AC Foods

The Challenge

AC Foods, a diversified food manufacturing company operating multiple distinct product lines, faced a common but critical challenge: leadership lacked a single view of performance across the business. Each product line operated with its own reporting cadence, data formats, and KPI definitions, making cross-line comparison and portfolio-level decision-making nearly impossible.

Executives were spending hours each week piecing together reports from different teams, often working with data that was days or weeks old. The absence of standardized metrics and drill-down capability meant that strategic decisions were based on incomplete pictures and gut feel rather than comprehensive intelligence.

AC Foods needed a single source of truth that could serve executives with portfolio-level visibility while enabling drill-down into any product line, region, or time period—accessible through natural language, not SQL.

Business Impact

Unified Cross-Product Visibility

All product lines consolidated into a single executive dashboard with standardized KPIs and real-time performance data.

Natural Language Querying

Executives can ask questions like "What was our margin on Product Line A in Q3?" and receive instant, accurate answers.

Full Drill-Down Capability

From portfolio overview to individual SKU performance—leadership can explore data at any level of granularity without waiting for reports.

The Solution

North Labs built a unified data layer that ingested operational, financial, and sales data from each of AC Foods' product lines into a centralized warehouse. Standardized data models were created to enable apples-to-apples comparison across lines while preserving the nuance of each business unit's unique metrics.

On top of this foundation, an executive intelligence platform was deployed with three key capabilities: real-time dashboards with drill-down from portfolio level to individual product and SKU performance; natural language querying for ad-hoc questions; and automated alerting for KPI deviations and anomalies across any product line.

The platform was designed for self-service—enabling executives and product line leaders to explore data independently without submitting requests to a central analytics team.

The Outcome

AC Foods now operates with a unified executive intelligence platform that gives leadership real-time, drill-down visibility across all product lines. Natural language querying has eliminated the bottleneck of formal report requests, empowering leaders to ask questions and get answers in seconds.

The platform has improved strategic decision-making speed, enabled faster identification of underperforming segments, and created a culture of data-driven leadership across the organization. The engagement continues to expand into supply chain optimization and predictive demand forecasting.
